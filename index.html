Our Mission

The Responsible AI Lab (RAIL) is dedicated to bridging the gap between high-performance machine learning models and robust, equitable real-world applications. We focus on the sociotechnical implications of AI, ensuring that as systems become more autonomous, they remain aligned with human values and safety standards.

Research Pillars

Reliability & Robustness

Developing systems that perform safely under distributional shift and adversarial conditions. We move beyond standard benchmarks to test AI in dynamic, unpredictable environments.

Fairness & Ethics

Investigating algorithmic bias and developing mitigation strategies. We work on mathematical definitions of fairness that translate effectively to policy and social contexts.

Interpretability

Opening the "black box" of deep learning. Our work focuses on post-hoc explanation methods and inherently interpretable model architectures for high-stakes decision making.
